Your Home Above the Adriatic

Finished in spring 2026 — Brač stone, oak details and wide sliding windows with amazing views in the peaceful village of Murvica, Bol, island of Brac

Our villa is located in the quiet village of Murvica in the Bol municipality. Overlooking vineyards, the sea and the island of Hvar, you can enjoy a picturesque village with beautiful, unobstructed views. For spending quiet evenings without traffic, crowds of tourists or loud music, this is the perfect place.

The villa is a combination of Brač stone, oak wood details, wide sliding windows and modern materials. It was finished in spring 2026, so everything is completely new. Across three floors it accommodates 8 persons plus 2 babies in four spacious en-suite bedrooms — every one of them with unforgettable views straight from your bed.

Location

Murvica, Bol municipality — the sunny south side of the island of Brač

Villa Bila sits above the sea in the small village of Murvica, five kilometres west of Bol. The village overlooks vineyards, the Adriatic and the island of Hvar — an authentic, peaceful corner of Brač away from the summer crowds, yet minutes from the island's most famous sights.
